GLA UNIVERSITY MATHURA

Founded in 1991 by Shri Narayan Das Agrawal, GLA University Mathura was established in honor of his father, Late Shri Ganeshi Lal Agrawal. With the aim of providing quality education, the university stands as a center for learning in the sacred city of Mathura. Established under the Sri Jagannath Prasad Ganeshi Lal Bazaz Charitable Trust Samiti, GLA University has grown into one of the premier educational institutions in India.

GLA University has become a symbol of excellence by providing quality education in various disciplines including Engineering, Management, Law, Pharmacy, and the Sciences. The university is well-known for its focus on holistic student development, providing a nurturing environment that promotes the growth of students into globally competitive professionals.

Key Features of GLA University

  • Ranked among the top private universities in India with NAAC A+ accreditation.
  • World-class infrastructure with modern labs, research centers, and fully equipped classrooms.
  • State-of-the-art hostel facilities, sports complexes, and health centers.
  • International collaborations with top universities worldwide.
  • Dedicated training and placement cell with strong corporate relations.
  • Industry-integrated curriculum designed to prepare students for real-world challenges.

Why Choose GLA University?

At GLA University, we believe in transforming young minds into future leaders by focusing not just on academic excellence but also on skill development. With a strong focus on research, innovation, and entrepreneurship, GLA offers a dynamic learning environment. Our faculty comprises highly qualified professionals and industry experts, ensuring that students receive both theoretical knowledge and practical exposure.

Programs Offered

  • Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech)
  • Master of Business Administration (MBA)
  • Bachelor of Science (B.Sc)
  • Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm)
  • Master of Law (LL.M)
  • PhD Programs in various disciplines

Campus Life

The lush green campus of GLA University provides a vibrant and enriching environment for students. From state-of-the-art laboratories and libraries to excellent sports facilities, the university offers everything needed for the all-round development of its students. The university also organizes cultural festivals, technical events, and student clubs to encourage participation and enhance leadership skills.
